Scrambled eggs

This is my favourite food I've been making since I was a child. Simple yet delicious, and doesn't take too much time.

Ingredients

  • egg
  • oil
  • salt
  • more eggs

Steps

  1. put some oil in a pan on the stove 🛢
  2. when it sounds hot enough, crack an egg into it 🍳
  3. if it sizzles, crack all the eggs You'd like to into it 🥚🥚🥚
  4. keep stirring it until the expected softness is reached 🥣
  5. put it on a plate 🍽
  6. salt if needed 🧂
